Playing media files

When a piston's Play track uses an old-school x-file-cifs://… network-share URL, PistonCore streams the file to your speakers itself — the share is read straight from the URL, nothing to set up here. Files kept in Home Assistant that use /local/… play through HA directly and don't need this at all. How this works →
